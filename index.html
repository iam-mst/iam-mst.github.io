Additive manufacturing, often referred to as three-dimensional (3D) printing, is a way of making products and components from a digital model. It is being applied in a wide range of industries, including defense, aerospace, automotive, medical, and metals manufacturing.

This Laboratory conducts research aimed at meeting some of the major technical challenges of rapid product realization. Check Background Page where we have described the motivation and objectives of creating this research lab.

There are a variety of areas of research being studied by graduate and undergraduate students in the lab under the supervision of faculty members. Research Projects lists the current cutting edge research going on in our lab.
